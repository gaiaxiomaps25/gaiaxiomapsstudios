What is Quotes App – Motivation & Wisdom?

Quotes App – Motivation & Wisdom is a simple, powerful app that fills your day with inspiration. Whether you need a boost of motivation, wisdom, or love, this app delivers beautifully curated quotes right when you need them — with no distractions.
